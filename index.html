About Me

I'm Dan Chilia, a web and software developer based in Kenya, focused on building websites and practical software that solve real problems for small businesses and individuals. I work across the stack — from responsive, mobile-friendly front-ends to PHP/MySQL and Python-powered backends — and I enjoy turning everyday business processes like bookings, sales, and inventory into systems that people actually want to use.

My approach is simple: understand what the client actually needs, keep the code clean and maintainable, and communicate clearly from the first conversation to final delivery. I'm equally comfortable building a brochure-style website for a local business or a custom management system with a database behind it, and I'm always learning new tools to solve problems more efficiently.

When I'm not coding, I'm usually exploring new frameworks, contributing to personal projects, or refining the systems I've already built based on real user feedback.
